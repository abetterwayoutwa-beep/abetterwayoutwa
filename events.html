Homeless Outreach

📍 Downtown Bellingham — Cornwall Alleys 🕒 Meet at 12:00 PM 🤝 Open to All Volunteers

Join us on April 11th as we serve those experiencing homelessness in Downtown Bellingham. Volunteers will meet at 12:00 PM to prepare food together, then head out to the Cornwall Alleys area to distribute meals and supplies directly to those in need.

No experience necessary — just a willing heart and a few hours of your time. Come ready to cook, connect, and make a real difference in our community.

Q&A with the Director

📍 Ferndale Public Library — 2125 Main St, Ferndale, WA 98248 🕒 10:30 AM - 12:30 PM 👥 Ages 12+

This is your chance to:

  • Ask questions about our mission
  • Meet the director and our dedicated team
